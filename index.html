Projects

  • Guidance Beat: Responsive Footwear for Visually Impaired people

    The Smart Shoe for Blind People is an innovative assistive device designed to enhance the mobility and safety of visually impaired individuals. This smart shoe integrates advanced technologies such as ultrasonic sensors, vibration motors, and microcontrollers to detect obstacles and provide real-time feedback to the user. Ultrasonic sensors, strategically placed on the shoe, emit waves that bounce back from obstacles, allowing the shoe to detect objects in the user's path accurately. When an obstacle is detected, vibration motors embedded in the shoe activate, providing haptic feedback to alert the user. This hands-free, intuitive navigation aid significantly improves the user's independence and confidence while moving around, ensuring a safer and more navigable environment for visually impaired individuals.
